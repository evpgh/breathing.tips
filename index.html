top 5 breathing tips

    anti-stress

    Resonant coherent breathing or controlled slow breathing improves HRV and blood pressure and decreases stress.[1][2][3] A calming practice that places the heart, lungs, and circulation into a state of coherence, where the systems of the body are working at peak efficiency. Inhaling and exhaling at a rhytm of 5.5 seconds is shown to be optimal.[4]

    relax deeply

    This deeply relaxing technique was introduced by Dr. Andrew Weil - called 4-7-8 breathing technique, it is effective in reducing dyspnea, anxiety and depression[5]
