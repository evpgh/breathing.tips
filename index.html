top 3 breathing tips

    Wake up

    Inhale for 6, exhale for 2 seconds. Repeat 5 times. Fill your lungs with fresh new air to fire up the blood circulating your brain.

    40 sec

    Rest

    Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat 6 times. By exhaling long and loud you tell your body it's time to relax.

    2 min

    Perform

    Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4 seconds. Repeat 4 times. Pumps you up.

    1 min
